Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Century Holidays, we recognize the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book travel services, or interact with us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Data: Full name, date of birth, gender, passport details, and nationality.
  • Contact Data: Billing address, delivery address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Financial Data: Bank account, payment card details, and billing information (processed securely through our partners).
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments to and from you, and other details of products and services you have purchased from us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to facilitate our services. This includes:

  • Processing your travel bookings, reservations, and ticketing.
  • Communicating with you regarding your itinerary, updates, and emergency notifications.
  • Improving our website functionality and customer service experience.
  • Sending promotional marketing materials (only if you have opted in).

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

In order to fulfill your travel arrangements, we must share specific required personal data (such as your name and passport details) with third-party service providers. This includes airlines, hotels, tour operators, transport companies, and relevant government authorities (for visas and customs). We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties for their independent marketing use.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of industry-standard security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. However, no absolute guarantee of security can be provided for data transmission over the Internet.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also have the right to object to the processing of your data or request data portability. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
